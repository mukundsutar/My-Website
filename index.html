Tic Tac Toe Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe, also known as noughts and crosses or Xs and Os, is a simple two-player game typically played with a pencil and paper. The playing board consists of a 3x3 grid, and each player takes turns placing their symbol (traditionally X for the first player and O for the second player) in an empty square on the board. The objective of the game is to be the first player to get three of their symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, while also preventing the opponent from doing the same. If all the squares on the board are filled and no player has won, the game is a draw.

Wordle Wordle

Wordle is a word-guessing game played online. In the game, the player has six chances to guess a five-letter word chosen at random by the game. The player makes a guess by typing in a five-letter word, and the game indicates which letters in the guess are in the correct position in the target word, and which letters are present in the word but in the wrong position. Based on this feedback, the player can make subsequent guesses to narrow down the possibilities until they correctly guess the target word or run out of guesses. The game is won when the player correctly guesses the word within the allotted number of tries.
